Overview

Located near the adventure hub of Manali in Himachal Pradesh, the bhirgu Lake Trek is a short yet rewarding high-altitude experience. Ideal for beginners and seasoned trekkers alike, the trail ascends to a breathtaking 13,700 ft within just a few days.

The journey starts from Gulaba, just a short drive from Manali, and takes you through open grasslands, oak forests, and mountain ridgelines. The star attraction— bhirgu Lake—holds mythological significance and offers serene reflections of surrounding peaks. Perfect for long weekends or short escapes, this trek delivers maximum mountain drama with minimum days on the trail.

Itinerary

You’ve switched your charity’s office to renewable energy. You’ve cut most flights. The office fridge is stacked high with oat milk, and lycra-clad staff are proudly showing off their subsidised bicycles. On your way to net-zero, right? But operational emissions are just one part of the net-zero picture: your money, too, has a carbon cost attached.

Day 1: Manali to Gulaba (Drive) and Trek to Gulaba Meadows (Camp)

  • Altitude:
    • Manali: 2,050 m / 6,750 ft
    • Gulaba: 3,000 m / 9,900 ft
    • Gulaba Meadows: 3,200 m / 10,400 ft
  • Distance:
    • Drive: 21 km (1–2 hrs)
    • Trek: 500 meters (approx. 20 minutes)
    • Details: Reach Manali and drive to Gulaba. From there, a short acclimatization hike brings you to your first campsite amid meadows.

Day 2: Gulaba to Rola Kholi

  • Altitude:
    • Gulaba: 3,000 m / 9,900 ft
    • Rola Kholi: 3,800 m / 12,500 ft
  • Distance: 6 km | Approx. 4 hrs
  • Details:Trek through alpine forests and meadows with gradual elevation gain. Camp at the scenic Rola Kholi base.

Day 3: Rola Kholi to bhirgu Lake and Back

  • Altitude:
    • Rola Kholi: 3,800 m / 12,500 ft
    • bhirgu Lake: 4,200 m / 13,700 ft
  • Distance:: 10 km | 8 hrs (round trip)
  • Details: Early start to reach bhirgu Lake, passing steep ridgelines. Spend time by the lake before returning to camp.

Day 4: Rola Kholi to Gulaba and Drive to Manali

  • Altitude:
    • Rola Kholi: 3,800 m / 12,500 ft
    • Gulaba: 3,000 m / 9,900 ft
  • Distance:
    • Trek: 6–7 km | 3 hrs
    • Drive: 21 km to Manali | 1–2 hrs
  • Details: Descend back to Gulaba and drive to Manali by afternoon for onward travel.

Trek Highlights

  • High Altitude Alpine Lake: bhirgu Lake sits majestically at 4,200 meters and is considered sacred by locals. It remains semi-frozen even during summer.
  • 360° Mountain Views:Panoramic views of snow-clad peaks from the Pir Panjal and Dhauladhar ranges accompany you throughout.
  • Accessible Yet Stunning:Just a short drive from Manali, this trek is perfect for those seeking high-altitude thrills in a short timeframe.
  • Meadows to Marvel At: Walk through wide-open alpine grasslands that resemble those in European countryside—lush, green, and dotted with wildflowers.
  • Cultural Blend Interact with Gaddi shepherds and learn about local Himalayan traditions on the trail.
