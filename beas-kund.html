Overview

The Beas Kund Trek is one of Himachal Pradesh’s most iconic short treks, offering a perfect mix of spiritual lore, scenic beauty, and adventure. Starting from Manali, this trek takes you deep into the heart of the Dhauladhar Range, following the Beas River to its source—Beas Kund, a high-altitude glacial lake at 3,900 meters.

In just a few days, trekkers pass through scenic meadows, cross glacier-fed streams, and witness the grandeur of snow-capped giants up close. With its relatively easy trail and high reward-to-effort ratio, Beas Kund is an ideal weekend or beginner’s trek for those seeking a taste of the Himalayas.

Itinerary

You’ve switched your charity’s office to renewable energy. You’ve cut most flights. The office fridge is stacked high with oat milk, and lycra-clad staff are proudly showing off their subsidised bicycles. On your way to net-zero, right? But operational emissions are just one part of the net-zero picture: your money, too, has a carbon cost attached.

Day 1: Drive from Manali to Dundi, Trek to Palchhani Thach

  • Altitude:
    • Manali: 2,000 m / 6,400 ft
    • Dundi: 2,800 m / 9,300 ft
    • Palchhani Thach: 3,100 m / 10,100 ft
  • Distance:
    • Drive: 22 km |1 hr
    • Trek: Trek: 2–3 km | 2 hrs.
  • Details: Begin your journey from Manali and drive to Dundi. Trek to the peaceful alpine grasslands of Palchhani Thach for your first camp.

Day 2: Trek from Palchhani Thach to Lohali

  • Altitude:
    • Palchhani Thach: 3,100 m / 10,100 ft
    • Lohali: 3,400 m / 11,100 ft
  • Trek Distance:: 3–4 km | 4–5 hrs
  • Details:A gradual ascent brings you to Lohali, a beautiful campsite set against rocky ridges and mountain views.

Day 3: Trek from Lohali to Summit Point via Lady Leg and back

  • Altitude:
    • Lohali: 3,400 m / 11,100 ft
    • Lady Leg: 3,900 m / 12,700 ft
    • Beas Kund Summit Point: 3,900 m / 12,800 ft
  • Trek Distance:: 5–6 km | 5–6 hrs
  • Details: Today’s highlight is reaching Beas Kund. Trek via the steep Lady Leg ridge to witness the emerald lake and towering glaciers, then return to Lohali.

Day 4: Lohali to Dundi and Drive Back to Manali

  • Altitude:
    • Lohali: 3,400 m / 11,100 ft
    • Dundi: 2,800 m / 9,300 ft
  • Distance:
    • Trek: 5–6 km | 4–5 hrs
    • Drive: 22 km | 1 hr
  • Details: Descend through the valley to Dundi and drive back to Manali by afternoon.

Trek Highlights

  • Sacred Glacial Lake: Visit the legendary Beas Kund—the origin of the Beas River, where Rishi Vyas is believed to have meditated.
  • Panoramic Views: Marvel at towering peaks like Hanuman Tibba, Ladakhi, Friendship Peak, and Shitidhar, all around 6,000 meters.
  • Accessible High-Altitude Trek: Ideal for beginners, this short trek from Manali takes you to alpine meadows and glacial basins in just 4 days.
  • Lush Meadows & Streams: Trek through the serene landscapes of Dundi and Palchhani Thach, with wildflowers, grazing sheep, and pristine brooks.
  • Thrilling Glacier Viewpoints: Climb toward the Lady Leg ridge for dramatic glacier and valley views that reward your effort.
